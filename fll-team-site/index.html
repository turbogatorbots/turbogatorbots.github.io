What Is First Lego League?

First Lego League is an international robotics competition where students design, build, and program LEGO robots to complete missions. Teams also learn research, presentation, innovation, and teamwork.

Why We Love It

  • We get to build and program robots.
  • We solve tough challenges together.
  • We learn by testing, improving, and trying again.
  • We grow through teamwork, creativity, and persistence.
