# ABE Graduate Applications — preliminary review (FA26 active root-folder applicants)

Date: 2026-03-16
Reviewer: Berry (preliminary machine-assisted screen)

## Scope and caveat

This is an initial screening review of the **62 PDF applications in the root of**:
`Agricultural and Biological Engineering - Graduate Applications`

I treated the root-level PDFs as the current active pool and did **not** include archived/cancelled/denied subfolders in rankings. This review is useful for **triage and shortlist formation**, but it is not a substitute for final faculty review of full packets, letters, and transcripts.

Main limitations:
- Some PDFs extract text cleanly; a few older package-style PDFs extract poorly.
- GPA/test score parsing is incomplete for some files.
- I prioritized evidence of **research readiness, fit to ABE themes, publication/output record, technical depth, and specificity of faculty/research alignment**.

## What I looked for

For each applicant, I weighted:
1. Research fit to ABE / likely lab fit
2. Evidence of sustained research experience
3. Publications/conference output or strong project portfolio
4. Technical skills relevant to the stated area (e.g., ML/remote sensing/hydrology/robotics/bioprocessing)
5. Academic preparation (when clearly visible)
6. Clarity/specificity of purpose statement
7. Any obvious concerns (fit mismatch, weak signal, conduct flags, older/deferred status, unclear materials)
